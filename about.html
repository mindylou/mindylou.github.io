Welcome to my website! I am a rising sophomore from Massachusetts in Cornell University's College of Engineering. I am planning on majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Business. Outside of class, I am a member of Women in Computing at Cornell, Engineering Ambassadors, Club Swimming, and Engineers Without Borders. In my free time, I love to try new food, collect stickers, and watch The Mindy Project (no relation). I am interested in interactive experiences that combine my passion for helping others with my skills in computer science. This summer, I was a teaching assistant for Girls Who Code's Summer Immersion Program in the Washington, DC area.
